The Best Moives by Pranav

My Top 3 moives of all time

URI:THE SURGICAL STRIKE

This moive is all about the planning and execution of the retaliation and revenge India gave back to Pakistan by destroying almost 5-7 of their terrorist camps by sending in 4 teams of Para special forces for the task.

THE LONE SURVIVOR

This moive is about how 4 US navy seals were sent on an operation but due to some unavoidable circumstances the terroists get to know about them and 3 of them eventually die fighting back but 1 seal survives in the hard terrain for 10 days after which he is evacuated by the US army by launching a full operation.

YEH JAWANI HE DIWANI

This moive is about 4 friends who go on a trek to Manali where they have the best time of their lives but due to carrer choices they have to part ways at the end of the trip leading to arguments and misunderstandings. But they eventually meet and reunite after many years for their best friend's wedding.
